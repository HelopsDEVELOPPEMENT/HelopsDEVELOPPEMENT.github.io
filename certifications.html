SAP Certifications

SAP defines two levels of certification:

  • Associate Certifications: Verify fundamental knowledge and target profiles with less than 3 years of experience in the area.
  • Professional Certifications: Require proven project experience, business process knowledge, and a more detailed understanding of SAP solutions. Target profiles with minimum 4 - 7 years of experience in the area.
